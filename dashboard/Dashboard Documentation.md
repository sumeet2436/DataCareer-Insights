# 📊 Dashboard Documentation

## Dashboard Overview

The Power BI dashboard provides insights into global Data Science, Analytics, and Data Engineering job market trends.

---

## KPI Cards

### Total Jobs

Displays the total number of job records available for analysis.

**Value:** 565

### Average Salary

Displays the average salary across all job postings.

**Value:** $110.61K

### Remote Jobs

Displays the total number of fully remote jobs.

**Value:** 346

### Medium Companies

Displays the number of job opportunities posted by medium-sized companies.

**Value:** 290

---

## Visualizations

### Salary Growth by Experience Level

Shows how average salary changes across experience levels:

* Executive
* Senior
* Mid
* Entry

**Key Insight:** Executive-level positions receive the highest compensation.

---

### Work Mode Distribution

Displays job distribution across:

* Remote
* Hybrid
* Onsite

**Key Insight:** Remote jobs account for the largest share of opportunities.

---

### Company Size Distribution

Displays job opportunities across:

* Small Companies
* Medium Companies
* Large Companies

**Key Insight:** Medium-sized companies dominate hiring activity.

---

## Business Insights

* Executive roles earn the highest salaries.
* Remote jobs represent the largest work category.
* Medium-sized companies post the highest number of openings.
* Salary increases significantly with experience level.

Provide actionable insights into salary trends, work modes, company sizes, and career opportunities within the global data industry.
